About This Calculator

This calculator determines the cash premium for sugar based on:

  • NY No. 11 futures price (cents/lb)
  • FOB Cash premium (cents/lb)
  • Freight costs (USD/mt)
  • Other costs (USD/mt)
  • Fixed conversion factor: 22.0462 (converting cents/lb to USD/mt)

The calculation follows the same logic as the Excel sheet, computing the CNF price and subtracting the converted futures price.
